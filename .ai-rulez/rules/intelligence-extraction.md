# Intelligence Extraction

- **Deep Research:** Prioritize founder philosophy, technical roots, and funding signals. Be aware that founder philosophy is less relevant for larger organisations and instead focus on known vision/plans for the next 6/12/24 months.
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Utilize Trustpilot/G2 data to identify "The Friction" (e.g., Price Creep, Communication Gaps).
- **Interview Strategy:** Generate high-signal, role-specific questions for C-suite vs. Engineering leads.
