# Data Policy

All data extraction and presentation must prioritize:

1. **Precision:** No hallucinations for dates, legal terms, or numbers.
2. **Auditability:** Every claim must link to a source (Trustpilot, JD, Founder Video).
3. **Governance:** Explicitly state all assumptions in strategic docs.
4. **Security:** No PII or credentials in logs or public reports.
